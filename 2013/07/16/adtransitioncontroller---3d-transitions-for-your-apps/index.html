ADTransitionController - 3D transitions for your apps

July 16, 2013

Romain Goyet, Pierre Felgines and I wrote ADTransitionController, a handy library that lets you easily add custom 3D transitions to any app. You can use it either with the transition APIs introduced by iOS 7 or as a full replacement for UINavigationController.

To see a demo of ADTransitionController, check out the video on its dedicated GitHub page.
